# Business Understanding

## Project Overview

The **AI-Assisted Analytics Platform** is an end-to-end Data Analytics portfolio project designed to simulate a real-world business analytics workflow. The project demonstrates how raw business data can be transformed into meaningful insights using Python, SQL, Power BI, and Artificial Intelligence.

The platform focuses on analyzing retail sales data to identify business trends, measure key performance indicators (KPIs), and generate actionable insights for decision-makers. In addition to traditional analytics, the project integrates AI to automatically generate business summaries and reports, showcasing modern analytics capabilities used in today's organizations.

## Business Problem

Retail organizations generate large volumes of sales, customer, and product data every day. However, this data is often stored across multiple sources, making it difficult for business stakeholders to quickly monitor performance, identify trends, and make informed decisions.

Without a centralized analytics solution, business users spend significant time preparing reports manually, which can lead to delays, inconsistencies, and missed business opportunities.

The objective of this project is to address these challenges by building an AI-Assisted Analytics Platform that automates data analysis, calculates key business metrics, visualizes performance through interactive dashboards, and generates AI-powered business insights for faster decision-making.


## Business Objectives

The primary objectives of the AI-Assisted Analytics Platform are:

- Develop a centralized analytics platform to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights.
- Automate data cleaning and preprocessing using Python to improve data quality and reduce manual effort.
- Analyze business performance by calculating key performance indicators (KPIs) using SQL.
- Design interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional performance.
- Integrate Artificial Intelligence (AI) to automatically generate business summaries and actionable insights.
- Automate report generation to provide stakeholders with consistent and timely business reports.
- Follow industry-standard development practices by maintaining a well-structured GitHub repository with comprehensive documentation.
- Build a resume-ready portfolio project that demonstrates practical skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, AI integration, and business analytics.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) will be used to evaluate business performance throughout the project.

| KPI | Description | Business Value |
|-----|-------------|----------------|
| Total Revenue | Total sales revenue generated during the selected period | Measures overall business performance |
| Total Orders | Total number of customer orders | Tracks business activity and demand |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | Average revenue generated per order | Measures customer spending behavior |
| Total Customers | Total unique customers who placed orders | Indicates customer base growth |
| Monthly Sales Growth | Percentage increase or decrease in monthly sales | Monitors business growth trends |
| Top-Selling Products | Products generating the highest sales revenue | Helps identify best-performing products |
| Sales by Region | Revenue generated across different regions | Supports regional performance analysis |
| Customer Segmentation | Classification of customers based on purchasing behavior | Helps marketing and customer engagement strategies |
| Category Performance | Sales performance across product categories | Identifies high-performing and low-performing categories |
| Customer Retention | Percentage of repeat customers | Measures customer loyalty and retention |

## Project Scope

### In Scope

The project includes the following functionalities:

- Data collection and understanding
- Data cleaning and preprocessing using Python
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL-based KPI calculation and business analysis
- Interactive Power BI dashboard development
- AI-powered business insight generation
- Automated report generation
- Professional project documentation
- GitHub repository with industry-standard structure
- Interview-ready documentation and project explanation

### Out of Scope

The following functionalities are not included in this project:

- Machine Learning model development
- Sales forecasting and predictive analytics
- Inventory optimization
- Real-time data streaming
- Web or mobile application development
- Cloud deployment and production infrastructure
- User authentication and role-based access control
